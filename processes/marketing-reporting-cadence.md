# Marketing Reporting Cadence

A structured framework for how Marketing Operations reports on performance
— what gets measured, how often, and who receives it.

---

## 🎯 Purpose

A reporting cadence ensures:
- Stakeholders always have visibility into marketing performance
- Decisions are made on data, not gut feeling
- Teams stay aligned on goals and progress
- Issues are caught early and acted on quickly

---

## 📅 Reporting Schedule

### Daily Reports
**Audience:** Marketing Manager, Paid Media Team
**Delivery:** Automated CRM/dashboard alert

| Metric | Source |
|---|---|
| New leads generated | CRM |
| Ad spend vs budget | Google Ads / LinkedIn |
| Form submission count | CRM / Website |
| Email deliverability issues | Email platform |

---

### Weekly Reports
**Audience:** Marketing Team, Sales Manager
**Delivery:** Email summary + CRM dashboard link

| Metric | Source |
|---|---|
| MQLs generated vs target | CRM |
| MQL to SQL conversion rate | CRM |
| Campaign performance summary | Ad platforms |
| Email campaign results | Email platform |
| Website traffic overview | Google Analytics |
| Lead source breakdown | CRM |

---

### Monthly Reports
**Audience:** Marketing Director, Sales Director, Leadership
**Delivery:** Slide deck + live dashboard walkthrough

| Metric | Source |
|---|---|
| Total leads & MQLs vs target | CRM |
| Pipeline contributed by Marketing | CRM |
| Cost per lead by channel | Ad platforms + CRM |
| Campaign ROI summary | CRM + Finance |
| Lead quality score trends | CRM |
| SLA compliance (Marketing & Sales) | CRM |
| Top performing content/assets | CRM + Analytics |

---

### Quarterly Reports
**Audience:** Executive Team, Board (if applicable)
**Delivery:** Formal presentation

| Metric | Source |
|---|---|
| Marketing sourced revenue | CRM + Finance |
| Pipeline contribution % | CRM |
| Channel attribution breakdown | CRM |
| Budget utilization | Finance |
| Goal vs actuals review | CRM |
| Next quarter targets & strategy | Marketing Plan |

---

## 📊 Core Metrics Glossary

| Term | Definition |
|---|---|
| MQL | Marketing Qualified Lead — a lead that meets scoring threshold |
| SQL | Sales Qualified Lead — accepted by Sales for outreach |
| CPL | Cost Per Lead — total spend divided by leads generated |
| Conversion Rate | % of leads that move to the next funnel stage |
| Pipeline Contribution | Revenue opportunities sourced by Marketing |
| Attribution | Identifying which channels/campaigns drove a conversion |

---

## 🛠 Tools & Data Sources

| Tool | Purpose |
|---|---|
| CRM (HubSpot/Salesforce) | Lead data, pipeline, SLA tracking |
| Google Analytics | Website traffic and behavior |
| Google Ads / LinkedIn | Paid campaign performance |
| Email Platform | Open rates, clicks, deliverability |
| Google Sheets / BI Tool | Data consolidation and visualization |

---

## ✅ Reporting Best Practices

- [ ] Always compare metrics to the previous period AND to target
- [ ] Highlight wins AND areas of concern — never just the good news
- [ ] Include a "So what?" insight for every key metric
- [ ] Keep executive reports visual — charts over
